# Fractional CMO Positioning

## The Shift

Lucas is transitioning from **"Pinterest Guy"** / **Marketing Consultant** to **Fractional CMO**.

## Why This Matters

### Marketing Consultant
- Seen as tactical ("do my ads")
- Hourly/project-based pricing
- Replaceable
- Limited scope

### Fractional CMO
- Strategic partner
- Value-based pricing
- Essential to business
- Owns entire growth function

## Key Differentiators

1. **Strategic Ownership**
   - Not just executing campaigns
   - Defining growth strategy
   - Making key business decisions

2. **Integrated Team Member**
   - Acts as part of the leadership team
   - Attends strategy meetings
   - Influences product direction

3. **Results-Aligned**
   - Compensation tied to business outcomes
   - Invested in client's success
   - Long-term partnership mindset

## Current Clients

### Blue Sportswear (5 years)
- Originally: Facebook/Pinterest/Google ads
- Now: Website development + CRO focus
- Goal: Increase conversion rate

### Fulcrum (Malaysia)
- Building website
- Transitioning to marketing
- Position: Essential fractional CMO role

## Pricing Evolution

| Stage | Model | Rate |
|-------|-------|------|
| Before | Hourly | Variable |
| Transition | Project | Fixed scope |
| Target | Retainer | 20,000 DKK/month per client |

## Goal Structure

**Target:** 3 clients Ã— 20,000 DKK = 60,000 DKK/month
- 4 hours/day max
- One-stop shop (marketing + web dev)
- Hire Teja (designer) full-time
- Hire Shopify developer

## Key Messaging

> "I don't just do marketing. I own your growth function."

## Action Items

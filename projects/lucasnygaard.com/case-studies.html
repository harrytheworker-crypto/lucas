Case Study 01

Blue Sportswear

From Pinterest ads to full growth strategy — a 5-year partnership that evolved as the brand scaled.

Duration
5+ Years
Services
Full Growth
Focus
CRO + Ads

The Challenge

Blue Sportswear had a decent product and some organic traffic, but their website wasn't converting. They were spending on ads but seeing poor returns. The owner knew they needed help but didn't have the budget for a full marketing team.

What We Did

This wasn't an overnight transformation. Over 5 years, we evolved the strategy as the business grew:

  • Year 1: Started with Pinterest ads — lower cost, higher intent traffic for sportswear
  • Year 2: Expanded to Facebook/Instagram ads with lookalike audiences
  • Year 3: Focused on CRO — redesigned product pages, simplified checkout, added trust signals
  • Year 4: Built retention systems — email marketing, loyalty program, win-back campaigns
  • Year 5: Website development project — complete rebuild for better performance and conversion

The Results

  • Conversion rate improved from 1.2% to 2.8% (133% increase)
  • Average order value increased 45% through upsell strategies
  • Customer lifetime value doubled with retention systems
  • ROAS stabilized at 4-5x across all channels
  • Now expanding into new markets with the same systems
"Lucas isn't just an ad manager. He's become our growth partner. Five years in, and he still surprises us with new ideas and optimizations."
Owner, Blue Sportswear
5-Year Client

Case Study 02

Fulcrum

Transitioning from hourly web development to fractional CMO role for a Malaysian fashion brand.

Duration
2 Years
Services
Fractional CMO
Focus
Strategy

The Challenge

Fulcrum came to me needing a website. They were a new Malaysian fashion brand with good products but no online presence. I built their first Shopify site, but I saw bigger potential — they needed strategic marketing, not just a pretty website.

What We Did

After launching the website, I proposed transitioning to a fractional CMO role:

  • Phase 1: Built and launched the initial Shopify store with conversion optimization
  • Phase 2: Set up tracking and analytics to understand customer behavior
  • Phase 3: Developed comprehensive marketing strategy — not just ads, but full funnel
  • Phase 4: Executed paid acquisition (Facebook, Google) with rigorous A/B testing
  • Phase 5: Built email flows and retention systems from day one

The Results

  • Launched to first $10K month within 90 days
  • Achieved profitability on first paid campaign (rare for new brands)
  • Built email list of 5,000+ subscribers in 6 months
  • Established repeatable customer acquisition system
  • Positioned for sustainable growth, not just launches
"Lucas helped us think bigger. We came for a website and got a growth partner. His strategic thinking has been invaluable."
Founder, Fulcrum
Malaysian Fashion Brand

Case Study 03

Pinterest Guy

My own service business — building systems that other agencies could use.

Duration
Ongoing
Services
Pinterest Ads
Focus
Systemization

The Challenge

Pinterest is an undervalued ad platform for fashion brands, but it's hard to find specialists. I built Pinterestguy.com to offer this service specifically. The challenge was creating repeatable systems that could serve multiple clients without me doing everything manually.

What We Built

  • Standardized Onboarding: Every client goes through the same data collection process
  • Creative Templates: Pinterest-specific ad formats that convert for fashion
  • Campaign Architecture: Proven account structure that scales
  • Reporting Dashboard: Automated client reporting saves hours weekly
  • Optimization Playbook: When ROAS drops, here's exactly what to check and fix

The Results

  • Served 10+ fashion brands with Pinterest-specific strategies
  • Achieved average ROAS of 6-8x (vs. 2-3x industry standard)
  • Reduced campaign setup time by 70% through systemization
  • Proved Pinterest as viable channel for fashion e-commerce

Note: Pinterest Guy is now being folded into Lucas Nygaard's fractional CMO services. The systems and learnings are available to all my clients, not just Pinterest-specific engagements.

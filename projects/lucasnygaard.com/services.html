Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a Growth Audit and Fractional CMO?

A Growth Audit is a one-time deep dive where I analyze everything and give you a roadmap. Fractional CMO is ongoing — I actually execute the strategy, manage campaigns, and optimize continuously.

Is there a minimum commitment?

For Fractional CMO, no minimum commitment — but I recommend at least 3 months to see real results. For the Complete Package, there is a 3-month minimum because of the website development work.

How is "1-1.5 hours per day" structured?

It's flexible. Some days I'll spend more time on deep work (like CRO), other days it's quick check-ins and optimizations. I track my time and you'll always know what I'm working on.

Do you work with non-fashion brands?

My expertise is specifically in fashion e-commerce. That's where I've spent 5+ years and gotten the best results. For non-fashion brands, I may not be the right fit.

What time zone are you in?

I'm in Bali, Indonesia (GMT+8). This works well for European clients (morning overlap) and Asian clients (afternoon overlap). For US clients, we can schedule calls accordingly.

How do I know if I'm ready for a Fractional CMO?

You're typically ready if: (1) You're doing $50K+ in monthly revenue, (2) You have some marketing in place but it's not optimized, (3) You need strategic guidance but can't justify a full-time CMO yet.
